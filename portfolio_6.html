Cloud Light Installation

material: LED lights, paint & tracing paper

above, lighting diagram

The use of paint over the LED lights enables a mild lighting color change from cool white to warm yellow, rose pink, peaceful blue or whatever color is desired and painted. Tracing paper, a transluscent and relatively stiff material, creates organic forms to enclose the LED lights and helps the colored lighting to diffuse into space.

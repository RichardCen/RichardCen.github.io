Design

The Zhenqin Portfolio Website utilizes Bootstrap front-end web framework. The Home page is divided up into five sections: featured projects slide shows, portfolio, design process, about me and contact.

above, zhenqin website flow

Vistors are welcomed by selected project drawings as they come to the website, which gives them the first impression and helps them understand the function of this portfolio webiste: to display works.

above, home slide show section wireframe

As they scroll down or clicks on the portfolio button in the navigation bar, visitors come to the second section, which is also the main section in this website: Portfolio. The portfolio section is divided to three categories: website design, architecture and research, and product & graphic design. Once visitors click on the images, they will be redirected to the individual project webpage.

above, portfolio section wireframe
above, portfolio webpage example

For each project webpage, there is always project information listed on the right corner of the page. Each project is explained by the combination of images including renderings, diagrams, drawings, and texts.

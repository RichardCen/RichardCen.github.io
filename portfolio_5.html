Stacking the View
project: graphic design

The project is to design a figure representing the Altamira Building in Argentina and the figure on the left is emphasized on the stacking properties embedded in the Altamira Building. There can be multiple interpretations assigned to the grey bars such as stacked floors or beams. The blue bars represent the views that are encountered by residents and are stacked into this matrix.

Toward Localization
project: graphic design

The project is to design a figure describing a shift from universality to specificity in architectural field. Clorindo Testa is one of these architects moving toward localization. While the greys on the left represent Clorindo's international style projects from 1950s to 1970s, the colored blocks represent his embracement of regionalism, particularly the use of colors, from 1980s.
